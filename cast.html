Descriptive Analysis

Since the MCU is creating a universe of super hero movies, it is common that they have casted actor’s multiple times for their film, it is so that fans would enjoy their favorite heroes reoccur in the MCU movies. A bar graph was used to easily see the difference in the casting of MCU’s main cast in their films. The most casted actors are Chris Evans, Samuel L. Jackson, and Robert Downey jr., who was casted in a total of 11 movies. With the Avengers cast following next as Scarlett Johansson and Chris Hemsworth both was casted in 9 movies. And the whole Gang of The Guardians of the Galaxy was mostly casted together in 6 MCU films. The end of the Avengers franchise has now opened up a new set of superheroes that is now being casted for their first movies for the phase 4 of MCU movies, some of them are big actors such as Angelina Jolie, Salma Hayek, Kit Harington and many others.
