Movie Rating per Year

Descriptive Analysis

The line graph shows the annual reviews that the MCU movies garnered throughout the years. A line graph was used to easily represent and visualize the progress of the reviews of MCU movies. As we can see in the graph the movies created by MCU receives a consistently high ratings especially in the recent years from 2016 up to 2019, the ratings almost remained at 90. It can also be seen that there was a rollercoaster of ratings in the years 2012- 2014, where it was at 91 in 2012 then went down to 73 in 2013, and in the following year it went back at 91 in 2014. The graph also shows that the ratings peaked at 94 in the year 2008, the beginning of its phase 1 films, but in the following year they were not able to follow up with the high ratings for in the year 2010 the ratings was at its lowest at 72.
